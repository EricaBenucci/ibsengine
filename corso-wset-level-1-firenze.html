The WSET Level 1 Award in Beer provides a great introduction to the world of beer. Learning through sight, smell and taste, you'll become familiar with a range of beer styles and how they are made.

If you work with beer, you’ll be able to answer customer queries with confidence. If you’re learning for fun, you’ll feel inspired to explore new styles and know how to get the most out of every glass.

What's covered?

  • Main ingredientsmalt, water, hops, yeast and other sugar sources
  • Production stepsmalting and brewing process
  • Storage & Serviceglassware, temperature, keg pouring and freshness
  • Beer StylesMalt-driven, Hop-driven (IPAs, Pilsners), Yeast-driven (Weissbier) and Sours
  • Food PairingHow flavors interact with different dishes
